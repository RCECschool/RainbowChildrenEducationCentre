A Few Words About Us

Rainbow Children Education Centre is a High School that designed for ambitious students in Myanmar and across the globe

Explore their intellectual interests based on ability, not age. Benefit from instruction by teachers with an average of 20+ years of experience. Study in small class sizes (no larger than 12 per class), and get the benefit of real-time virtually private instruction in a classroom setting. Graduate with university-recognised qualifications in K-12 Online School.

K–12 PROGRAMS & COURSES

Rainbow Children Education Centre offer quality learning in a non-traditional environment for students in grades K—12. Whatever brings your child to Rainbow, our commitment to student success remains the same. Explore the benefits of flexible online courses and the convenience of an online learning environment for your student with one physical instructor in each class.
